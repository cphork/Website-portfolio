ABOUT ME

Born and raised in San Francisco, CA I have adapted a mindset of "everything beautiful". I attended an institute, FIDM - Fashion Institue of Design & Merchandising, and finished the program with a degree in Visual Communication. Everything Beautfiul!

I enjoyed it very much but knew in my heart that something was missing. Years later after creating a career in hospitality from the bottom up I knew that I wanted something more. Something that I can create and deliver to the mass. After long deliberation among my friends and colleagues I decided to attend a bootcamp to become a FullStack Developer!

I've enjoyed every moment, through the treacherous moments and the magnificient ones. I am proud to say as of 06.22.2021 I am a FullStack Developer and am enjoying the road I have chosen.
